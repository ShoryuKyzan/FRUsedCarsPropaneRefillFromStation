description=
description=This project provides a mod for Project Zomboid that extends Filibuster Rhymes Used Cars & TiRekS Propane Upgraded.
description=
description=## Features
description=* refilling propane trucks (Franklin EF70) from a propane station
description=* refilling Industrial Propane Tanks (LargePropaneTank) from the truck and station
description=* refilling Propane Storage Tanks (HugePropaneTank) from the truck
description=* use the vehicle menu or context menu to do these actions.
description=
description=## Help
description=* For filling tanks from the truck, make sure they are in inventory/on ground/in a container next to you when you access the vehicle menu.
description=
description=# Requirements/Server Load order

description= (and other Filibuster Rhymes used cars mod ids required to activate this mod in the first place)
description=* FRUsedCarsPropaneRefillFromStation (this mod)
description=  * For servers, place this in the list after the other requirements
